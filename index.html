What is Educomm?

Educomm is an Educational Communication System, it is just like website or web app will help to maintain, create, and modify the school, colleges, and other private coaching institutes’ database. And help them to maintain the information of their institutes. They can create the database about their respective institutes that can include their students’ history like their marks, report card, attendance etc, faculties information , events updates, study materials etc information that required by the respected institutes. It will acts like Management System that will helps to maintains respected institutes, organization information.
